About

Your photo

I'm a product designer and front-end developer who believes great interfaces should feel inevitable — like they couldn't have been built any other way.

Based in the Philippines, I work with startups and established brands globally to build products that are both functional and memorable. My process starts with understanding the people who'll use the product, then working backward to the code.

When I'm not building things, I'm writing about design systems, typography, and the philosophy of making software that respects its users.
